Work With Us

RELIABLE CACFP & SFSP CATERING
FOR SCHOOLS AND YOUTH PROGRAMS

Serving the Atlanta metro with scratch-cooked meals, full compliance support, and on-time delivery — so you can focus on your students.

Running a CACFP or SFSP program means managing a lot. Reimbursement claims, meal pattern requirements, documentation, delivery logistics — and at the center of it all, making sure children actually get fed.

We partner with schools, afterschool programs, daycares, and summer sites across Gwinnett County and the greater Atlanta area to provide fully compliant meal service. Our team handles menu planning, preparation, and delivery. Every meal meets federal meal pattern requirements and is documented for your records.

What You Get
  • Scratch-cooked breakfasts and lunches
  • CACFP and SFSP compliant menus
  • Reliable on-time delivery to your site
  • Documentation support for reimbursement claims
  • A team with years of real program experience
Who We Serve
Schools, daycares, afterschool programs, summer feeding sites
Service Area
Gwinnett County and surrounding Atlanta metro

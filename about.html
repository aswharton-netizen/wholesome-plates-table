Who We Are

OUR
STORY

Wholesome Plates Catering LLC is a Black-owned food business built in Atlanta, Georgia — on the belief that great food, operational excellence, and generational wealth are not mutually exclusive.

Mission

WHAT
WE
ARE
HERE
TO DO

To feed people well, build generational wealth, and prove that Black-owned food businesses can operate at the highest level.

This is not a mission statement written for a grant application. It is the reason this company exists. Every meal we serve, every contract we sign, every team member we develop is proof that this standard is possible — and that it belongs to us.

Vision

WHERE
WE
ARE
GOING

A billion-dollar hospitality brand rooted in culture, technique, and community — built from Atlanta, recognized everywhere.

We are not building a catering company. We are building an institution. One that creates careers, not just jobs. One that earns its seat at every table — not because it was invited, but because the work demands it.

What Drives Us

CULTURE.
TECHNIQUE.
COMMUNITY.

C

Culture

Everything we cook is informed by tradition, identity, and pride. Culture is not a marketing angle — it is the foundation of how we approach every menu, every event, every plate.

T

Technique

We hold ourselves to a professional standard. CACFP and SFSP certified, trained in food safety, and committed to continuous improvement — because the people we feed deserve nothing less.

C

Community

We serve Atlanta's schools, youth programs, and community organizations. Feeding people well is not a transaction — it is a responsibility we take seriously, every single day.

Leadership

ARIANA WHARTON

Ariana Wharton founded Wholesome Plates Catering LLC with a simple conviction: that food can be a vehicle for wealth, dignity, and lasting change. Starting in Atlanta, she built a company that operates with the discipline of a enterprise and the soul of a community kitchen.

Her approach is direct and personal — high standards, deep accountability, and a genuine investment in the people who do the work. Every system, every hire, and every meal served reflects that commitment.

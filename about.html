About Us

WHO WE ARE

Every Child Deserves a Real Meal.

For too many kids in our communities, the meal they receive at school or in an afterschool program isn't a supplement — it's the meal. When food insecurity is part of a child's daily reality, it shows up in the classroom, in their ability to focus, and in their sense of stability. We started Wholesome Plates because no child should have to wonder where their next meal is coming from.

We bring over three years of CACFP and SFSP operational experience, serving hundreds of children daily across multiple sites in the Atlanta metro. Our menus are made from real, whole ingredients — cooked from scratch with the same care you'd bring to your own kitchen. Real food, made with care.

Our team knows what compliance looks like day to day. We show up, we document, and we take the trust that schools and families place in us seriously. Feeding children well is not a small thing. We don't treat it like one.
